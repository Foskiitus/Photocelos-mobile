Como Chegar

The municipality of Barcelos has a central position in the Minho region, part of the shaft that connects Porto to Braga and Porto to Vigo. Access to this area is simple and clear. This position allows a closeness between various parts of the country and neighboring Spain. Among the main roads stand out for its strategic importance to A3, A11 and A28 linking the county to the main roads of national and international circulation as the A1, A24, Auto-lane motorway Atlantic and the Rias Baixas in both neighboring Galicia.

The train is also a valid option, since Barcelos has direct connections to Porto, Viana, Valencia, Vigo and Braga, by this means, which expands options arrival in Barcelos through this convenient means.

The aircraft is also a mode of transport to consider, since the quick connection to Oporto and Vigo facilitates its use.
