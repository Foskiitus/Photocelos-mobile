Heraldry

Description: - green shield, with a basket of gold between two silver flowerpots, encabadas the same, highlighted in black and aligned in bands; in chief a silver circle loaded with a black cross, and campaign, a heraldic source. Silver mural crown with three towers. Listel white, with the legend in black: "LIJÓ - BARCELOS".


Symbology:

Shield - As the fields of our parish are permanently covered with a green mantle, and the shield of the coat of the parish it is also covered in the same color.


Cross - Remember the Chapel of Santa Cruz. Legend has it that when a pilgrim moved to Santiago appeared drawn a cross on the ground.


Macetas - Is the art of stonework. Lijó lived in great masters in the art of stone work. His memory is invoked, not only in coat, through the flowerpots, as in many existing details in older homes in the parish.


Ball - The ball, with its interior filled with water, river is Lombao.


Flag - yellow. Cord and tassels of gold and green. Rod and cast gold.


Seal - in terms of the Act, with the caption: "Junta de Freguesia de Lijó - Barcelos".
