Chapel of Santa Cruz

The Chapel of Santa Cruz is located in place of Ribeiro, in the municipality of Barcelos. It was built in the nineteenth century, having been founded to honor and praise a miracle that happened there in 1843.
Reportedly, on Sunday, June 11, 1843, this parish was made ​​a siege or a procession of São Sebastião.
At the time the procession passed near the chapel of the saint, having stayed behind Constantino de Faria, Manuel Barbosa (name uncertain), all of this parish, saw a cross drawn on the floor. The following Sunday, some parishioners surrounded the place where it appeared with a cross any fence, because of the animals, and after 15 days, constituted a committee to do this by sending a chapel of planking in which they set a framework with Christ crucified under the invocation of the Lord of Mercy. Still celebrates the miracle then occurred.
