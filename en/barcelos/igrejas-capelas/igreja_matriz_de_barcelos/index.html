Church Matriz of Barcelos

Under the invocation of Santa Maria Maior, have been begun in the thirteenth century, been undergoing several transformations over the centuries. Among these are:

  • XV century - around 1464 Ferdinand I, Duke of Bragança became a collegiate church;
  • XVI century - on the initiative of D. Diogo de Sousa, archbishop of Braga, has undergone several changes;
  • XVIII century - the beginning of the century bell tower was added;
  • XX century - in the early years of the century a rosette for its main facade was added, and the interior, rebuilt the head, removed some side chapels, and the organ moved to a former altar.

Classified as a National Monument by Decree No. 14.425, published in DG No. 228, of 15 October 1927.1
